# 🎯 Simple Presentation Guide

## 📋 What This System Does (In Simple Terms)

**Problem:** Government projects often have corruption, delays, and no transparency.

**Solution:** Blockchain system that tracks every rupee and every step automatically.

## 🔄 How It Works (5 Simple Steps)

### Step 1: Government Creates Project
- Government puts money in smart contract (like a digital safe)
- Money is locked until work is completed
- Example: "Build Bridge - 100 ETH budget"

### Step 2: Contractor Does Work
- Contractor completes milestone (like foundation)
- Takes photos/videos as proof
- Uploads evidence to system
- Submits for verification

### Step 3: Oracles Verify Work
- Independent verifiers (oracles) check the work
- Could be IoT sensors, drones, human inspectors
- Multiple oracles must agree (like getting 2nd opinion)
- Only then work is "approved"

### Step 4: Public Can Vote
- Citizens get 3 days to review the work
- They can approve or veto (reject)
- If too many vetoes → dispute process
- If approved → automatic payment

### Step 5: Automatic Payment
- Smart contract automatically pays contractor
- No human intervention needed
- Complete transparency - everyone can see
- Permanent record on blockchain
